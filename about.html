Empowering underserved African businesses to join the global marketplace while creating cultural connections and a better quality of life for the African diaspora

More than a global marketplace

Founded in 2021, Cleaques is a global lifestyle platform that enables people from the African diaspora to securely exchange products and services, find and promote events, and send and donate money. As Nigerian immigrants ourselves, we've experienced tremendous challenges and hurdles when trying to order products from back home due to lack of a reliable platform that would allow us to confidently engage with small businesses back home and fellow Nigerian immigrants here in the US. So we decided to create one. After several conversations with other members of the diaspora, we found that being able to buy products and services was not enough. Expats also craved connection and community and were looking for a way to safely pay bills for family members back home and donate to disaster relief, pandemic relief and other humanitarian causes. We poured our energy into creating a platform that would meet all the core lifestyle needs of the African diaspora, or any diaspora for that matter

Our vision
Connecting global expat communities with their roots

We aim to establish a truly global and inclusive marketplace that will connect people to cultural products, services, events and communities in their neighborhood and back home. Our values
You deserve a transparent, reliable and fair marketplace

We believe that while most people are honest and well intentioned, vetting both buyers and sellers enables transparency and communication in transactions. Setting up safeguards, like buyer and seller vetting and escrow, is the best way to create a trusted marketplace where small businesses can flourish and buyers can shop with confidence.

Supporting women and youth artisans

Everyone deserves an opportunity to share their talents and skills with the world.

When we launched Cleaques, one of our biggest goals was to create a platform where buyers and sellers can work together confidently. As immigrants trying to do business with providers back home, we have sometimes been disappointed when our orders didn't make it or sellers just didn't communicate. Trying to buy something from someone across the globe was risky and unreliable.

We believe that while most people are honest and well intentioned, vetting both buyers and sellers enables transparency and communication in transactions. Setting up safeguards, like buyer and seller vetting and escrow, is the best way to create a trusted marketplace where small businesses can flourish and buyers can shop with confidence

Supporting talented creators in Africa

Today, it is still challenging for people in developing economies to earn money from their business or side hustle online. Cleaques connects talented creatives and artisans with passionate buyers on a global marketplace, giving them a platform to share and monetize their talents. By discovering, connecting, and promoting untapped creators in Africa, we enable them to make a contribution to global culture while earning a living.
